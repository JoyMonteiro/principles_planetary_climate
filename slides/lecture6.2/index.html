Atmospheric Radiative Transfer

Two-stream equations

\[\begin{aligned} \frac{d}{d\tau_\nu}I_+ &= - I_+ + \pi B(\nu, T(\tau_\nu))\\ \frac{d}{d\tau_\nu}I_- &= I_- - \pi B(\nu, T(\tau_\nu)) \end{aligned} \]

Solutions

\[\begin{aligned} I_+(\tau) &= I_+(0)e^{-\tau} + \int_0^\tau \pi B(T(\tau))e^{-(\tau - \tau')}d\tau'\\ I_-(\tau) &= I_-(\tau_\infty)e^{-(\tau_\infty - \tau)} + \int_\tau^{\tau_\infty} \pi B(T(\tau))e^{-(\tau - \tau')}d\tau' \end{aligned}\]

Solutions

  • Beers Law
  • Infinite Isothermal Medium
  • Finite Isothermal Slab
